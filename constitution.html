Article I ‚Äî Valoria and the UUR

Valoria is a proud and sovereign realm within the Union of United Realms (UUR). Valoria shall remain loyal to the UUR and contribute to its collective security and prosperity. In return, Valoria enjoys protection and recognition as a semi-autonomous member state.

Article II ‚Äî Autonomy and Governance

Valoria holds partial independence, retaining the right to create and enforce its own domestic laws consistent with UUR principles. The national government operates as a constitutional monarchy with:

  • The King as Head of State
  • Lord of Alter Hafen as Deputy to the King
  • Valorian Representative Council (VRC) elected by citizens

Article III ‚Äî Rights and Duties of Citizens

All citizens are equal before the law and enjoy fundamental rights to safety, freedom of expression, property, and fair trial. Employment and contribution to the realm are civic duties and marks of honor.
